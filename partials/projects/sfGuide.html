San Francisco Travel Guide Redesign


The purpose of this project was to design a book on a topic of choice. I decided to redesign a visitor's guide to San Francisco I found online.

The original booklet had informative content but a cluttered design that seemed to contradict the San Francisco culture I'm familiar with and wasn't too appealing to its target audience. With this in mind, I sought to create a more minimalist, organized version of the travel guide.
